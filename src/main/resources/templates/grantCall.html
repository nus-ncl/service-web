National Cybersecurity R&D Lab Grant Call 2021

Call for Proposals

The National Cybersecurity R&D Lab (NCL) is funded under National Cybersecurity R&D (NCR) Programme since 2015. NCL aims to provide support to the Singapore cybersecurity R&D community in terms of their research experimentation and testing needs and to develop NCL into a hub that facilitates the growth of a vibrant Singapore cybersecurity ecosystem.

NCL invites innovative proposals for NCL grant call 2021.The objective of the grant call is to enable NCL to reach out to a wider community to enhance its services and build in-depth capabilities. The services developed will enrich NCL test bed to serve the Singapore Cybersecurity R&D community better.

Please refer to the documents below for more information on the grant eligibility, project scope, funding, submission process and other details.
