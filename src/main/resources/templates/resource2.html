OVERVIEW

The CSA-National CyberSecurity RD Labs Scholarship (NCLS) aims to grow and develop a pool of cybersecurity professionals in Singapore. This programme is funded by the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore (CSA) due to the increasing demand for job roles requiring deep specialization in enterprise IT and cyber-physical systems.

 

ELIGIBILITY

This scholarship is open to students who:

  • Are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents
  • Are enrolled in NUS Masters programme focus on cyber security (full- or part-time).
